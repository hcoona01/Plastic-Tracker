05 — Reduction Tips

Small changes,
massive impact

🧴

Switch to Bar Products

Replace liquid shampoo and body wash with bars. Each person saves up to 11 bottles per year by making this one swap.

−11 bottles/year
🍶

Carry a Reusable Bottle

One reusable bottle eliminates 156 single-use plastic bottles annually. Fill it up, show up, save the ocean.

−156 bottles/year
🛒

Bring Your Own Bag

The average plastic bag is used for 12 minutes but takes 400 years to degrade. A tote bag changes the math forever.

−500 bags/year
🥡

Pack Your Own Lunch

Avoid disposable containers and cutlery by bringing meals from home in reusable containers every day.

−250 items/year
🧊

Buy in Bulk

Buying larger quantities dramatically reduces packaging per unit. Bulk stores let you refill, not rebuy.

−60% packaging
🌿

Vote with Your Wallet

Support plastic-free brands. Each purchase sends a powerful market signal toward a cleaner packaging future.

−100% packaging plastic
